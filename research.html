Research

Current Research Focus

Our lab focuses on the dynamics of the Indian summer monsoon, exploring its variability, long-term changes, and impacts using advanced climate models, data analysis, and machine learning approaches.

We investigate phenomena such as monsoon low-pressure systems, dry air intrusions, climate change impacts, and develop AI-based prediction systems to enhance forecasting skill and climate resilience.
