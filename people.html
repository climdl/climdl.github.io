Researchers and scholars driving climate science forward.

Dr. S. Sandeep

Associate Professor

Dr. Sandeep leads the Climate Dynamics Lab and specializes in Indian monsoon dynamics, climate modeling, and AI applications in atmospheric science. He is currently an Associate Professor at the Centre for Atmospheric Sciences, IIT Delhi, a position he has held since December 2022. He joined the Centre as an Assistant Professor in November 2017.
